Muhammed Yıldız is a Computer Engineering student at Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University and an Undergraduate Researcher at the Biological Data Science Lab, Hacettepe University. He is passionate about designing AI-powered tools to solve routine problems, with research interests in world models, physical AI, and large language models. His work spans from adapting state-of-the-art LLMs to protein generation to developing computer vision systems for aviation applications.
