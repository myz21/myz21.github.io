#Art


While I was studying dentistry, I wanted to understand the human body mechanically rather than just memorizing it. To better learn anatomy during my dentistry studies, I drew detailed anatomical and biological sketches.


At the same time, I also draw caricatures that give social messages to people.


I regularly share my artwork on my Instagram page delusional.myz.
